About Me

A little bit about me.

Hello! My name is Emily, but I often go by my username Animatingdragon online. I've been drawing since i was in kindergarden, but i've been focusing more on art and animation since around 2017. My favourite thing to draw is Dragons and cats.

The reason I have been focusing on art recently is for the enjoyment of making a coherant art peice that looks decent. My favourite step of the drawing process is shading, I like to shade. I chose the username "Animatingdragon" as well, i enjoy both animating and dragons.
